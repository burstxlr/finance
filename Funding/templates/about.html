About Us

The purpose of the DUT ScholarHub system is to combine grants, scholarships, bursaries, and financial aid into a single platform to streamline the process of finding financial assistance.

Who We Are

Dut ScholarHub is a dedicated website, developed in 2024, which has a comprehensive database of scholarships, grants, and financial assistance possibilities.

What We Do

  • Facilitates the scholarship search process by providing tailored suggestions based on students' profiles and academic backgrounds.
  • Help students obtain helpful materials, advice on how to apply, and support from both domestic and foreign funding sources to increase their chances of successfully funding their academic periods.
  • Enables students to fulfill their academic goals free from the weight of financial limitations, regardless of whether they are pursuing undergraduate, graduate, or professional education.
  • It does this by acting as a reliable friend in navigating the complex world of funding alternatives.

Our Team

We currently have a staff complement of 11 team members, consisting of 4 managers responsible several financial and administrative support.
